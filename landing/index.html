A Unified Platform for a Smarter Response

Automated EIDO Ingestion

Ingest and standardize Emergency Incident Data Objects (EIDO) from any source, including raw text, emails, and structured data feeds. Our system automatically extracts key information, saving valuable time and reducing manual data entry.

LLM-Powered Geocoding

Pinpoint incident locations with unparalleled accuracy. Our Large Language Models can understand and geolocate addresses from unstructured text, even with incomplete or ambiguous information.

Intelligent Incident Correlation

Our IDX agent automatically identifies and links related incidents, providing a clear and comprehensive operational picture. Reduce clutter, eliminate duplicate efforts, and ensure a coordinated response.

Cross-PSAP Coordination

Break down communication silos between Public Safety Answering Points (PSAPs). Share critical incident data seamlessly and securely, enabling a truly unified response across jurisdictions.

Real-time Alerting & Notifications

Stay informed with customizable, real-time alerts. Configure triggers based on incident type, location, or severity, and receive notifications through your preferred channels.

Advanced Analytics & Reporting

Gain valuable insights into your operations with our powerful analytics dashboard. Track key metrics, identify trends, and generate comprehensive reports to improve future responses.

About Us

We are a team of researchers and engineers at the San Diego Supercomputer Center (SDSC) dedicated to improving emergency response through cutting-edge technology. The SDSC Orchestrator is an open-source project aimed at providing a powerful, flexible, and accessible platform for public safety agencies of all sizes.
